# Web-Scraping-ENDES-2004-2024
In Stata, this script provides an efficient and comprehensive way to download, organize, and process Peru’s Demographic and Health Survey (ENDES) for all available years.

The code automates the download of all survey modules directly from official sources. In addition, it extracts compressed (.zip) files, enabling a streamlined and reproducible data pipeline. However, in cases where the compressed files contain issues or inconsistencies, the extraction process must be performed manually.

### Modules

This script includes the treatment of the following modules:

1. **Module 1629** – Household Characteristics
2. **Module 1630** – Housing Characteristics
3. **Module 1631** – Basic MEF Data
4. **Module 1632** – Birth History – Method Knowledge Table
5. **Module 1633** – Pregnancy, Childbirth, Postpartum and Breastfeeding
6. **Module 1634** – Immunization and Health
7. **Module 1635** – Nuptiality, Fertility, Spouse and Woman
8. **Module 1636** – Knowledge of AIDS and Condom Use
9. **Module 1637** – Maternal Mortality and Domestic Violence
10. **Module 1638** – Weight and Height – Anemia
11. **Module 1639** – Child Discipline
12. **Module 1640** – Health Survey
13. **Module 1641** – Social Programs

Overall, this tool enables the construction of a harmonized database for demographic and health analysis, facilitating the monitoring of key indicators such as maternal and child health, nutrition, fertility, and social conditions, as well as supporting public policy evaluation in Peru.
